Tips for going to Dominican Republic, Punta Cana

First of all, it's good to try new things and activities such as the Banana boat! But if you're going on the Banana boat, you shouldn't get a baby on the Banana boat, because the baby can fall in the water and I don't think you'll want that to happen! Also when you're going on the Banana boat,hold onto the bars tightly and remember to take yourself a swimming costume so you don't get wet. Second, it's very hot so you shouldn't wear thick clothes or something that has long sleeves! But be careful because it rains almost every day here so bringing a jacket might be a good idea(the problem is that I forgot to bring my jacket so I can't go outside.) My opinion is that the ice cream labeled mix is the best one. Though I'm not sure completely because l've never tried the vanilla ice cream! The glass boat is great and the part I like best is when you get to go into the water!
